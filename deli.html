Delicious Chocolate Chip Cookies

Preparation time:30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter,softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teasoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl,cream together the butter and sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time,then stir in the vanilla.
  4. In a separate bwol,combine the flour and baking soda;gradually add to the butter mixture.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by roumded tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
